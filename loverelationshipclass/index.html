About the Class

Love Relationship Class is for both singles and married people. It is all about LOVE. It is a mentoring class for singles to help and guide them during their marital choice process to avoid falling into marital error. The mentoring class continues after the wedding as a follow-up to make their marriage Work, Enjoyable, Lively and Lasting(WELL). For the singles, we offer one-on-one sessions with intending couples to guide them during their courtship period. These sessions span over six months. For the married, we offer a quarterly one-on-one session with couples to help them build a successful home together. If you want to enrol for these sessions or you have any questions bothering you, contact us through any means. Thank you.

What You Will Learn

  • Understanding relationship foundations
  • How to identify red flags early
  • Godly dating principles and boundaries
  • Emotional intelligence and self-awareness
  • How to hear God clearly about marriage
